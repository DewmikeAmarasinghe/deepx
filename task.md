# Task

You are a research analyst. Investigate the current state of open-source LLM inference
frameworks — specifically Ollama, vLLM, and llama.cpp.

For each framework:
- Find its current version and when it was last updated
- Find the key hardware requirements and what GPUs it supports
- Find one real benchmark or performance number published in the last 6 months
- Find one major limitation or known issue raised by users

Then compare the three side by side and write a recommendation for a developer who
wants to self-host a 7B parameter model on a single consumer GPU (RTX 3090 or similar).

Save your research notes to research/ as you go.
Write the final comparison and recommendation to output/inference_frameworks.md.
Keep the final report under 500 words and make it opinionated — pick a winner.
